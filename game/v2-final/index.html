How to Play

Lactose. The most dangerous cause of death known to man. Fight your milk and get enough calcium or die trying.

Your goal: Achieve a calcium score of 5

Be careful! If your health hits 0, you will die of lactose overdose.

Take Lactaid

The slow game. If you decide to take a lactaid and:
1. The milk is lactose: you gain +1 calcium points.
2. The milk is lactose-free: you lose -1 health.

Drink Milk

The fast game. If you decide to drink the milk and:
1. The milk is lactose: you lose -2 health.
2. The milk is lactose-free: you gain +2 health.

Choose your gameplay wisely, as strategy is crucial to your survival. Good luck!
